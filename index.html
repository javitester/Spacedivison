About Us

Team Zenit is a student-run organization focused on advancing aerospace engineering at Universidad Europea de Madrid. We aim to innovate and inspire the next generation of aerospace engineers.

Our Projects

Astraeus I

The Astraeus I is Team Zenit's inaugural project. Launched in early 2024, this carbon-fiber rocket achieved the L1 rocketry certification, setting the foundation for future innovations in rocket design.

Astraeus II

Building upon the Astraeus I, the Astraeus II incorporates improvements to enhance performance. The project was launched on October 20, 2024, with the goal of achieving greater stability and reliability.

Vrackie I

Vrackie I is our latest project, utilizing lessons learned from Astraeus I and II. This rocket will be equipped with advanced electronics and aims to achieve an L2 certification using a solid motor for greater propulsion.
