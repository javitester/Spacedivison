About Us

Team Zenit is a student-run aerospace club dedicated to advancing knowledge and passion for aerospace engineering and aviation. Our mission is to innovate, educate, and inspire the next generation of aerospace enthusiasts.

Our Projects

From model rocketry to drone technology, Team Zenit is at the forefront of hands-on aerospace projects. Our members work collaboratively to design, build, and test innovative solutions.

Upcoming Events

Join us for workshops, seminars, and competitions that bring together enthusiasts and professionals from the aerospace industry.
