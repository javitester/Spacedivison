About Us

Team Zenit is a student-run aerospace club at Universidad Europea de Madrid, passionate about advancing aerospace engineering. We innovate, educate, and inspire the future of aerospace technology.

Our Projects

Astraeus I

The Astraeus I was the inaugural rocket project of Team Zenit. Built entirely from carbon fiber, it successfully achieved L1 certification by completing a successful launch and landing. This milestone marks our club's technical capabilities and our commitment to the development of rocketry.

Astraeus II

The Astraeus II project builds upon Astraeus I by refurbishing its body with enhancements like an I motor and advanced electronics. The project culminated in a successful launch in October 2024, demonstrating further technical advancements.

Vrackie I

Vrackie I is an enhanced iteration of our previous rockets, utilizing lessons learned from Astraeus I and II. Its goal is to achieve L2 certification using a powerful solid motor and further refine our design process.
